Background

Trained in classical piano theory and performance since 6 years old, Seth has taught multiple students, hosting one-on-one personalized lessons with books set for a comfortable and challenging pace to help his students grow in performance and musical theory.

Repertoire

Scheduling

Whether your child has never laid hands on a piano, or has prior experience, Seth will tailor each lesson to their needs and help them acheive their goals.

See calendar below for available times and scheduling. Appointments are half an hour at $15 per lesson in studio, or at your home at $20 per lesson.

A one time-purchase of a piano book will be required after the first lesson. Most books will range from $20-$30 for a year's worth of bookwork.
